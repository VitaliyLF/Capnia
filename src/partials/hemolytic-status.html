"Hemolysis-Related Carbon Monoxide Monitoring with CoSense: A Diagnostic Aid"

CoSense is not intended to screen or diagnose a specific disease or condition. Rather, it is a tool intended for the monitoring of carbon monoxide in medical conditions in which the rate of hemolysis may be relevant.

US Indication for Use: The CoSense® ETCO Monitor is indicated for the monitoring of carbon monoxide from endogenous sources (including hemolysis) and exogenous sources (including CO poisoning and smoke inhalation) in exhaled breath. The end tidal carbon monoxide level can be used for the monitoring of carbon monoxide in medical conditions in which the rate of hemolysis may be relevant.
